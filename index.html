07 — Questions

Before you write in

How do I join the IB programme?

Message the partner desk on WhatsApp. A specialist activates your IB account and issues your referral link in the same conversation.

How much does an IB earn?

Up to 60% revenue share on your referred clients' trading volume, with no cap on the number of clients you bring in.

Can I fund my account with UPI or crypto?

Yes. Both are processed instantly — UPI through GPay, PhonePe or Paytm, and crypto through USDT, BTC or ETH.

Can I withdraw directly in INR?

Yes, via UPI, net banking, IMPS/NEFT/RTGS, or debit and credit cards — no conversion step.

How long do withdrawals take?

UPI and crypto withdrawals clear quickly. Bank transfers settle within one to three business days.

Are my funds and data secure?

Client funds are held in segregated accounts, and the platform runs on industry-standard SSL encryption throughout.
